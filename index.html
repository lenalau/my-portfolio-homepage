ABOUT ME


Coming from the humanities (studying language, culture and society for over ten years), I am relatively fresh in the tech-scene.
I took part in a part-time web-development bootcamp in 2019 which equiped me with the basic skills of contemporary web-development. Since this was just the beginning of a fascinating and fulfilling journey, I am now looking for an employer, who gives me the chance to deepen, strengthen and advance my programming skills. I am a curious, loyal and fast-learning person, putting 100% commitment and dedication into my work, my personal and professional development as well as into my projects and my team.


Born and raised in a flat part of western Germany, the Münsterland, I moved to Berlin in 2009 by choice. Since then I built my base here. This city inspired me developing in various directions and explore more fields - apart from my initial profession. Recently I realized that coding has become a big passion to me. Although I like experiencing other places (I lived and worked in Vienna (Austria), as well as in Barranquilla (Colombia)) my base is here, in Berlin. That's why I am looking for a job in Berlin.
I speak GERMAN as my native language, as well as fluently ENGLISH and communicative SPANISH.


My interests are sports, photography ( have a look at my photoblog) and spending some good time in the nature or with friends.
